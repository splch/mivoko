Why it's built this way

Words are taught in corpus-frequency order and scheduled by FSRS-7 (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler), which targets your chosen retention rate. Review prompts ask you to produce the word from its meaning — retrieval practice transfers to real use. Personas are instructed to elicit self-correction (prompts) rather than silently recasting errors, and to weave your due words into conversation.
